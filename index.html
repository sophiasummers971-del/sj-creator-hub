chapter vii

YOUR GUIDE

Step-by-step. Plain English. No tech degree needed.

1
Download this file to your phone or PC
Tap the download button above. Save the file called jade_creator_os.html somewhere you'll find it — like your Downloads folder or Desktop.

Open it in Chrome or Firefox. Not Safari, not Samsung Browser — Chrome works best for everything.
✦ The whole app is this one file. No internet connection needed once downloaded (except for AI features).
easy
2
Customise your intro screen
On the first screen (the cinematic one with your big title), look for the small panel in the top right corner. Type your name, your tagline, anything you want. Hit Apply and watch it change live.
✦ This is your OS — make it yours.
easy
3
Turn on sound
Top left corner — small button that says ♪ off. Click it once. You'll hear a deep cinematic ambient drone. That's your working soundtrack. Click again to toggle off.
✦ Sound uses Web Audio — no files, just maths. Works offline.
easy
4
Use the Command Hub (dot 2)
Tap the second dot at the bottom, or click "Command Hub" on the intro screen. This is your AI prompt library.

Tap any card to open it. See the small gold pills? Tap one and it sends that prompt directly to Claude in the chat. Replace words in [brackets] with your actual content first.
✦ Works best when Claude.ai is open in another tab.
easy
5
Generate startup ideas with the Idea Lab (dot 3)
Go to dot 3. Type a niche or space you're interested in — for example: "AI tools for solo creators" or "mental health apps for Gen Z".

Pick a mode: Lean means cheap and solo. VC-Backable means big and scalable. Trend-Led means riding a hot wave. Hyper-Niche means small and specific.

Hit ✦ Generate Ideas and Claude creates 5 startup concepts live, with names, taglines and descriptions.
✦ Needs Chrome + internet. The ideas are generated by Claude AI in real time.
medium
6
Edit your Blueprint numbers (dot 4)
Go to dot 4. The Blueprint shows your income projections. See those gold numbers at the top? They're editable — just tap and type your own figures. Hit Save Blueprint Changes and the charts update instantly.

The stream cards at the bottom also have editable notes — tap the grey text to update.
✦ The charts rebuild automatically when you save. Use your real numbers.
easy
7
Connect your Inbox (dot 5)
Go to dot 5. You need three things: your IMAP server address, your email, and an App Password.

For Gmail: Go to myaccount.google.com → Security → 2-Step Verification (must be on) → App Passwords → Create one. Copy the 16-character code.

IMAP host for Gmail: imap.gmail.comIMAP host for Outlook: outlook.office365.com
Enter these in the Inbox screen and hit Connect.

Your social DMs (Reddit, Instagram, Telegram, X) are simulated from your bot's data — in the future these wire directly from your Python bot's JSONL log file.
✦ The OS never stores your password. It only exists in memory while the page is open.
medium
8
Use the AI Mirror (dot 6)
Go to dot 6. This is the most powerful screen. Paste anything — a post you saw, an email you received, a rough idea, a caption you drafted — then pick a mode:

My Voice — rewrites it sounding like you
Analyse — breaks down why it works or doesn't
Amplify — makes it 10x more powerful
Hook It — rewrites the opening to stop the scroll
Caption — turns it into a social media caption
Thread — explodes it into a Twitter/X thread
Cold Email — turns it into an outreach email

Hit Mirror It. Claude transforms your content live. Every output saves in the history below so you build a personal library over time.
✦ The more you use it, the more useful it becomes. Your history stays as long as the page is open.
medium
9
Run the Python bots in Termux
Your bots run on your Android phone inside Termux. Here's the order to do it:

Step 1 — Install Termux from F-Droid (not Play Store)
Step 2 — Run these commands one by one: pkg update && pkg upgrade pkg install python termux-setup-storage pip install requests schedule Step 3 — Copy your bot .py files into Termux storage
Step 4 — Run the bot: python your_bot.py Leave Termux open in the background. The bot keeps running.
✦ If the bot stops when your screen locks, install Termux:Boot from F-Droid to auto-start it.
advanced
10
Connect everything — the full loop
When everything is running, this is your daily routine:

🌅 Morning — Open the OS, go to Inbox, hit Refresh. See what collabs, subs and fan messages came in overnight. Reply using AI Draft for speed.

🧠 Creating — Use the AI Mirror to transform any content idea into captions, hooks, threads or emails in seconds.

💡 Ideas — When you spot a trend, go to Idea Lab, describe the space, generate 5 startup angles in 30 seconds.

📊 Monthly — Update your Blueprint numbers with your real income. Watch the charts show your actual growth.

🤖 Background — Your Python bot handles the platforms automatically. The OS is your command view on top.
✦ You built this. Now run it every day and it compounds.
easy
